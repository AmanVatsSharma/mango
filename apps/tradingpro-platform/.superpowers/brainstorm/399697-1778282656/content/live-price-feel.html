How should live price updates feel?

When a new tick arrives on the watchlist, what's the right visual response?

A

Flash & Fade

Price background flashes green (uptick) or red (downtick) for 400ms then fades back. Classic Bloomberg / Kite behaviour — instant directional read.

B

Glow Row

The entire card row gets a subtle green/red glow on the left border for 600ms. Less aggressive than a flash — the whole row communicates direction, not just the price digit.

C

Flash + Staleness Indicator

Flash on tick (like A), but also show a subtle ⚡/🔴 dot when the WebSocket feed is live vs stale. Traders know at a glance whether they can trust the prices.
